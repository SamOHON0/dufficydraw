The Draw
Each entry holds three nations. A team's points come from the furthest stage it reaches. Scores update automatically from the live feed.

How scoring works

A team collects the points for every stage it clears, added together. Reaching the Round of 32 is worth 1. If that same team goes on to the Last 16 it adds 2 more (3 total), and so on up to 21 for a tournament winner (1+2+3+4+5+6). Your entry's score is the sum across all three of your nations, so three teams that each go deep can beat one that wins it all.
